What is your Return Policy?

we understand that sometimes your dream item didn’t end up being all that dreamy. We are happy to accept returns and will issue store credit for the amount of the item(s). Please review our full return policy below. Returns are for store credit only. If item(s) arrive damaged, we will ship out a replacement if available, or store credit will be issued if item(s) are unavailable. Return shipping labels can be generated upon return through our online Returns Portal found above. Please be sure to select Redo Free Returns and Exchanges when ordering, and for a small fee of $3.98 in the US/$4.98 for Canada - your return label will be FREE if needed AND your package will be covered if lost, stolen, or damaged. Plus the exchange is automatic when selected! If Redo is not selected, you are still able to initiate a return, but at your cost of return shipping. Items marked FINAL SALE in title or on product page may not be returned, refunded or exchanged. This includes holiday doorbusters. We are always making room for new inventory! All returns/exchanges must be done within 30 days of order fulfilled. Items must be unwashed and unworn. If items appear worn, have makeup or other stains, excessive pet hair, or smell of smoke or perfume, your return WILL NOT be accepted and will be returned to you at your expense. NON-RETURNABLE: Final sale items, Jewelry & Accessories, Intimates, Bras, Camisoles, Slips, Socks, Hats, Holiday Doorbusters, and Health & Beauty items. Shoes must be returned in their original box or bag provided and placed inside an additional protective shipping safe box/bag to be eligible. Damages must be reported within 3 days of receiving merchandise. Please contact infoguzel.com .com or 317-888-8259 for assistance. Store credit expires 1 year after it being issued. Magnolia Boutique reserves the right to charge a flat $10 restocking fee for excessive returns. Exchanges excluded. Magnolia Boutique reserves the right to refuse service to customers who abuse our policies listed above.
